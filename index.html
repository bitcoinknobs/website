What is Bitcoin Knobs?

Bitcoin Knobs is a fork of Bitcoin Knots that takes flexibility a step further. We believe in maximum choice.

Why?

Because Node operators should be able to have maximum freedom to tune their node. We shouldn't allow trusted developers to dictate what users should and should not configure.

It's time we take our knobs into our own hands.
